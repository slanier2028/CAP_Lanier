My Future


Throughout the course, I learned how to create quizzes, apps, games, and websites which allows me to apply them to creative fields like art and animation. I developed my art skills and grew as an artist and animator after creating animations for sprites in game projects and by putting my drawings into my projects. I thought that I would struggle a lot when I was first introduced to coding, but I managed to find fun at learning how to code and how much of a benefit it would be in the future. I hope to use my computer science skills for future projects that I may work on later on in life such as creating a website for my art portfolio or possibly creating games since I am learning how to animate as well. I still experience some problems when it comes to making games or creating a website, but I can try solving these problems by learning new pieces of code that I can use with the help of others.
